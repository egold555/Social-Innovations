Our Mission

The Education for Inspiration Foundationâ€™s mission is very simple. We recognize that the spread of disease is both a medical and social issue. We strive educate our community on infectious diseases in the hopes of raising awareness. We also endeavor to correct any misinformation surrounding infectious diseases and their treatments. Through this we are trying to eliminate panic and provide resources for individuals in the community. This education will provide the building blocks for future innovation with the goal of stopping the spread of infectious diseases.
